Frequently asked questions
How do I reset my password? +
Open the Staff Art app and tap Log In. On the login screen, tap Forgot password? and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link shortly. If the email doesn't arrive within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact us at support@staffart.com.
How do I purchase artwork? +
Browse or search for a piece you love, then tap it to open the artwork detail page. Tap Buy and follow the secure checkout flow. Payment is processed safely through our payment provider. Once your purchase is confirmed, you'll receive a receipt by email and the artist will be notified to arrange delivery.
How do I contact an artist directly? +
Visit the artist's profile page and tap the Message button to start a conversation. You can ask questions about a specific artwork, discuss commissions, or enquire about shipping before making a purchase.
Can I sell my artwork on Staff Art? +
Yes. Create an account and set up your artist profile from within the app. Once your profile is complete, you can upload works, set prices, and publish them to the marketplace. You can also create and manage your own shows directly from the app.
How do I report an issue or inappropriate content? +
To report a specific artwork or user, tap the three-dot menu on their profile or artwork page and select Report. For technical issues or anything else, reach out directly to support@staffart.com with a brief description of the problem and your device model.
I'm not receiving email notifications — what should I do? +
First, check that notifications are enabled in Settings → Staff Art → Notifications on your iPhone. Also check your email spam/junk folder for any messages from us. If the problem persists, contact us at support@staffart.com and we'll look into your account.
